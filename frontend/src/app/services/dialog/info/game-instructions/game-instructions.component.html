Roles

  • api Super Admin: The "Super Admin" is the highest level of administrator on the website. Their primary function is to manage user roles and assign privileges. They have the authority to appoint moderators and grant them specific permissions. In gaming rooms, the Super Admin holds the same privileges as the room owner and is immune to the influence of other roles.

  • grade Moderator: The "Moderator" role has similar privileges to the "Super Admin" In gaming rooms, however, they possess the same privileges as the room owner. Moderators play a crucial role in enforcing rules, maintaining order, and ensuring a positive user experience on the website.

  • verified Owner: The "Owner" is the highest authority within their owned room. They have exclusive privileges limited to their specific room. As an owner, they can appoint admins with similar privileges, including the ability to silence, ban, and kick users from the room. Additionally, they can grant admin status to other users.

  • gamepad Admin room: The "Admin" role is appointed by the owner and possesses elevated privileges within the room. They can silence disruptive users, ban users from the room, and kick them out if necessary. While admins cannot appoint other admins, they play a vital role in maintaining order, enforcing rules, and assisting the owner in managing the room effectively.

Rules

When the ball reaches one of the game window's side limits, the player defending the opposite side wins a point.

The first player that wins 5 points wins the match.

Versions

There are two game versions.

  • Classic: Each player controls a paddle that can move up or down.

  • Hero: Each player controls a paddle that can move up or down, and a hero that can appear from the top or the bottom of the player's side. Each hero has unique trajectories and can be invoked once per point.
